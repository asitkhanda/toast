Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 28, 2026

This Privacy Policy describes how Toast (the macOS menu bar application available at toast.asit.space) and its website handle information. Toast is developed independently and is not affiliated with Vercel Inc.

Summary

Toast is designed to keep your data on your Mac. The app stores your Vercel personal access token and preferences locally. It communicates directly with Vercel’s API to read deployment status. Toast does not operate user accounts, analytics, or advertising on your behalf.

Information the app stores locally

On your Mac, Toast may store:

  • Your Vercel personal access token, saved in the macOS Keychain
  • Your selected team, watched projects, and app preferences (for example, polling settings, notification preferences, and background behavior)
  • Cached deployment and project data needed to display status in the menu bar

This information stays on your device unless you remove it by disconnecting the app or uninstalling Toast.

Information sent over the network

When you use Toast, the app may contact:

  • Vercel (vercel.com) — to validate your token and fetch teams, projects, and deployment status using read-only API requests
  • toast.asit.space — to check for app updates via the Sparkle appcast feed

Toast does not send your Vercel token to any server operated by the Toast developer. API requests go directly from your Mac to Vercel.

Website

The Toast website provides a landing page, download redirect, and update feed. Like most websites, hosting infrastructure may log standard request metadata (such as IP address, browser type, and requested URL) for security and operations. The site does not use third-party analytics or advertising trackers.

Notifications

If you enable notifications, Toast uses macOS local notifications to alert you when a watched deployment completes. Notification content is generated on your device from deployment data retrieved from Vercel.

What we do not collect

  • No Toast account or login is required
  • No usage analytics or crash reporting service is built into the app
  • No sale or sharing of personal information for advertising

Third-party services

Your use of Vercel is governed by Vercel’s privacy policy. Downloads are hosted on GitHub Releases; GitHub’s policies apply to that infrastructure.

Your choices

  • Disconnect your token at any time in Toast Settings
  • Remove the app and its Keychain entry by uninstalling Toast
  • Use a read-only, team-scoped Vercel token with the minimum access you need

Children

Toast is not directed at children under 13, and we do not knowingly collect their data.

Changes

We may update this policy from time to time. The “Last updated” date at the top of this page will reflect the latest revision.

Contact

Questions about this policy can be sent via GitHub Issues on the Toast repository.
